# Data Description

This folder contains the macroeconomic datasets used for the analysis and prediction of the USD/CAD exchange rate. 

The data includes multiple economic indicators that may influence currency movements between the United States and Canada.
## Dataset Files

The following datasets are included in this folder:

• CPI_Canada.csv  
  Consumer Price Index data for Canada, representing inflation levels over time.

• CPI_US.xlsx  
  Consumer Price Index data for the United States.

• GDP_Canada.csv  
  Historical Gross Domestic Product data for Canada.

• GDP_US.csv  
  Historical Gross Domestic Product data for the United States.

• Canada_Policy_Interest Rate.csv  
  Historical policy interest rate set by the Bank of Canada.

• US_Policy_Interest Rate.csv  
  Federal Reserve policy interest rate data.

• Oil_Rates.xls  
  Historical crude oil price data.

• Gold Futures Historical Data.csv  
  Historical gold futures prices.

• Trade Balance From Canada Side with US.csv  
  Trade balance data showing exports and imports between Canada and the United States.

  ## Data Sources

The datasets used in this project were collected from publicly available financial and economic data sources from both Canadian and United States institutions.

Sources include:

• Government economic statistics websites  
• Central bank databases (Bank of Canada and U.S. Federal Reserve)  
• Public financial market datasets  
• Economic data repositories such as FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data)

These sources provide reliable macroeconomic indicators used for financial and economic analysis.
